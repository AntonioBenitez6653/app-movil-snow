RIDER PROFILE

USA

Travis Rice

Widely considered the best all-around snowboarder in the world. Travis Rice has revolutionized the sport through his iconic films like "The Art of Flight" and "Dark Matter", and by creating the Natural Selection Tour.

He combines massive freestyle trickery with big mountain courage like no other. Travis is constantly searching for the perfect turn and the ultimate adventure, pushing the boundaries of snowboard exploration in the most remote corners of the globe.

His vision for snowboarding focuses on natural terrain features and deep powder, bridging the gap between competition and pure soul riding.
