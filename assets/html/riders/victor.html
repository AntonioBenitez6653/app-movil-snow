RIDER PROFILE

France
36 years old

Victor De Le Rue

With snowboarding in his blood (brother of legendary Xavier De Le Rue), Victor has carved his own path, becoming a two-time Freeride World Tour Champion. Known for his creative approach to the mountain and fearless freestyle integration into big mountain lines, he dominates both steep spines and massive kickers.

Victor skills from the slopes translate into his impressive catalog of video parts in some of the greatest snowboard films. With his board under his secure in March 2024, Victor became the most decorated male snowboarder in FWT history, solidifying his status as a legend of the sport.

Following in the footsteps of his legendary brother Xavier De Le Rue, Victor has forged his own path through insane creativity and fearless commitment. Whether chasing titles for competition, filming projects, or seeking personal adventures, his snowboarding comes from deep love for snow and the globe. Off the snow, he finds balance through surfing, paragliding, climbing, and mountaineering, embracing the mountains in every possible way.
